Welcome!


Welcome to your one stop shop for learning all about personal finance. This site is aimed at helping college students learn the basics of personal finance. It's an important life skill that most college students don't learn. This site aims to break down overwhelming and confusing financial concepts to make them easy to understand and implement.

This website will take you through the basics of personal finance, learning about:

  1. Interest and inflation
  2. Money management
  3. Investing
  4. Retirement

I recommend going in this order, as each section builds on the last one. Each section will have a "Basics" section for the basics. Then, if you want more info you can move to the "Deeper Dive" section. If you want even more information, you can proceed to the "Nitty Gritty" section. Each section will also have a "Resources" section where you can find resources and more information about a topic.
